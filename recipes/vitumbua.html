Vitumbua

This is a east african sweet delight called Vitumbua recipe. Its the perfect local sweet and tastes like nothing you have ever had.

Ingredients

  • 120g Oats
  • 50g Protein Powder of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 chopped banana for sweetness (optional)
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Add porridge to boil with water
  2. Add in protein powder
  3. Add in peanut butter and stir well
  4. Add more water as needed and chopped bananas
